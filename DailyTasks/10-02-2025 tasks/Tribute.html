Tribute to Team India

About

The T20 World Cup 2024 was an electrifying tournament that brought together the best cricketing nations in the shortest and most thrilling format of the game. **Co-hosted by the United States and the West Indies**, this edition marked a historic moment as the USA debuted as a host nation for a major ICC event. With an expanded lineup of **20 teams**, the tournament delivered intense competition, stunning performances, and unforgettable moments. Fans witnessed high-scoring games, dramatic finishes, and cricketing superstars showcasing their skills on the grand stage. As teams battled for supremacy, the **T20 World Cup 2024** became a landmark event in cricket history, further popularizing the sport across new regions and leaving a lasting legacy in global cricket.
